Placement Services

At Skriptx, we understand that training is just one part of the equation. That's why we go the extra mile by offering comprehensive placement services for both candidates and companies, bridging the gap between talent and opportunities in the tech industry

For Candidates:
  • Career Guidance: Receive personalized career counseling from our experts, who help you identify your strengths, goals, and career pathways.
  • Resume Building: Craft a standout resume that showcases your skills and experiences effectively, increasing your chances of catching the attention of employers.
  • Interview Preparation: Gain valuable insights and tips to excel in technical interviews, behavioral assessments, and case studies.
  • Job Matching: Leverage our extensive network of partner companies to find job opportunities that align with your skills, interests, and career aspirations.
  • Continuous Support: Benefit from ongoing support and guidance even after securing a job, ensuring a smooth transition into your new role.
For Companies:
  • Talent Acquisition: Tap into our pool of skilled and vetted candidates to find the perfect fit for your team, whether you're hiring for full-time positions, internships, or contract roles.
  • Customized Recruitment Solutions: Receive tailored recruitment solutions based on your company's unique needs, culture, and hiring requirements.
  • Streamlined Hiring Process: Save time and resources with our efficient hiring process, which includes pre-screening, technical assessments, and candidate shortlisting.
  • Dedicated Account Management: Partner with a dedicated account manager who serves as your point of contact throughout the recruitment process, ensuring a seamless experience from start to finish.
  • Post-Placement Support: We continue to support both candidates and companies even after the placement is made, ensuring a successful onboarding process and long-term fit.

Whether you're a candidate seeking your dream job or a company looking to build a stellar team, Skriptx is your trusted partner in navigating the complex world of tech recruitment. Let us help you unlock your full potential and achieve your goals.
